About BloodLink

Our Mission

BloodLink is a comprehensive blood bank management system designed to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients. Our mission is to ensure that no patient dies due to unavailability of blood.

How It Works

Step 1

Donor Registration

Individuals register as blood donors by providing their details and blood type.

Step 2

Blood Donation

Donors visit our centers to donate blood, which is then tested and stored.

Step 3

Blood Request

Hospitals or patients in need request specific blood types through our system.

Step 4

Matching & Delivery

We match requests with available blood and coordinate delivery to the recipient.

Blood Donation Facts

One donation can save up to three lives.
The entire donation process takes about 45 minutes to an hour.
You can donate whole blood every 56 days.
About 38% of the population is eligible to donate blood, but less than 10% actually do.
Blood cannot be manufactured—it can only come from volunteer donors.

Our Team

BloodLink is developed and maintained by a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, software engineers, and community volunteers committed to saving lives through technology.
